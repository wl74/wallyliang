Creative Statement

My name is Wally Liang, I'm from the Lower East Side of Manhattan. My work revolves around photography and photography based tools to invoke compelling ideas of beauty, familarity, and peacefulness, whilst also being an autobiography.

My main influences come from the entire planet, from hustling cities, to quiet farms, to the dangerous peaks of mountains, to the depths of the ocean. The world around me inspires to create, more than just one specific place like New York City, or Yellowstone National Park, or etc. The views and locations are what inspire me to create and achieve those ideas, as we often forget the world around us and the beauty within each of those areas. The other aspect of how we have memories and emotions that can't be told verbally but only be seen. Capturing moments of what I view goes to the autobiography, taking pictures of the small or quiet moments of my life as well.
